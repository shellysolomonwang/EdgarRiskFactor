OTHER INFORMATION On November 3, 2017, the Company entered into an amendment (the “Revolving Loan Amendment”) to the 2016 Revolving Facility. In addition, on November 3, 2017, the Company entered into an amendment (the “Term Loan Amendment”) to the 2016 Term Facility. The Revolving Loan Amendment and the Term Loan Amendment increased the maximum consolidated leverage ratio from 3.75 to 1.00 to 4.25 to 1.00 through the December 31, 2018 reporting period in the maximum consolidated leverage ratio financial covenant, which requires maintenance of a maximum ratio of consolidated total indebtedness as of the end of any quarter to consolidated EBITDA for the trailing four quarters (as defined in the 2016 Revolving Facility and 2016 Term Facility). The Company is in compliance with this covenant at September 30, 2017, and expects to remain in compliance for the next twelve months.
